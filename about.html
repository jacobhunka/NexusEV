OUR PRINCIPLES

A few things we believe strongly enough to write down:

  • 01 We link to the original source. Always. We are a curator, not a publisher. Every article heading is a direct link to the creator or outlet that did the actual work.
  • 02 We label sponsored content clearly. If a brand has paid to appear on this site, it is marked "Sponsored" without exception. Editorial coverage is never for sale.
  • 03 We cover the full picture. That means both the promise of EVs and their real limitations, both the case for solar and the honest payback timelines.
  • 04 We are not affiliated with any manufacturer. Rivian, Tesla, Hyundai, Toyota — they don't fund us and they don't influence what we cover.
