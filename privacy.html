This Privacy Policy explains how nexusEV ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit nexusev.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

nexusEV is a news aggregator. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to browse the Site. However, certain information may be collected automatically when you visit:

  • Log data — Your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and time spent on the Site, collected by our hosting provider.
  • Cookies — Small files placed on your device by us or third-party services (see below). You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Newsletter sign-up — If you subscribe to the Nexus Brief newsletter, we collect your email address to send you the newsletter. We do not sell or share email addresses with third parties.

GOOGLE ADSENSE & ADVERTISING

nexusEV uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you may opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

THIRD-PARTY LINKS

nexusEV is a news aggregator. Every article card on this site links to an external source — a YouTube video, a news article, or a publication. When you click a link and leave the Site, you are subject to the privacy policy of that third-party website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

We use YouTube thumbnail images (served via YouTube's image CDN) to display previews for video articles. These images are static and do not embed YouTube tracking cookies.

SPONSORED CONTENT

Some cards on this site are marked "Sponsored". These are paid placements by advertisers. Sponsored content is always clearly labeled and is never presented as independent editorial coverage. See our Advertise page for details.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
