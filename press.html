Events Calendar

Key European Shows & Events

These are the most relevant European automotive and EV events for nexusEVnews coverage. Shows marked Near Italy are within easy travel distance. Shows marked Top Priority are the highest-profile press opportunities. Shows marked EV Focus are specifically EV or clean energy oriented.

Event Date Location Notes
Motor Valley Fest
Near Italy Top Priority 		May 2026
Annual		 Modena, Italy 🇮🇹 Ferrari, Lamborghini, Pagani country. Panels, drive experiences, and brand exhibits in the heart of the Motor Valley. Italy-based — ideal home event. Press registration typically opens March–April.
Villa d'Este Concorso d'Eleganza
Near Italy 		May 2026
Annual		 Lake Como, Italy 🇮🇹 BMW Group-hosted prestigious concours at the Grand Hotel Villa d'Este. Concept cars and coachbuilt one-offs. Smaller event — approachable for independent press. Stunning location.
Euro EV Show
EV Focus Top Priority 		May 5–6, 2026 Madrid, Spain 🇪🇸 Europe's largest dedicated EV industry event. Specifically EV and clean energy — directly aligned with nexusEVnews coverage. Easy to justify as press. ~2hr flight from Italy.
EV & Hybrid Tech Expo Europe
EV Focus 		June 9–11, 2026 Stuttgart, Germany 🇩🇪 Battery tech, EV supply chain, OEM engineers. More technical than consumer-facing — strong Battery Tech and Deep Dives content opportunity. Short flight from Italy.
Goodwood Festival of Speed
Top Priority 		July 2026
Annual		 Chichester, UK 🇬🇧 One of the world's best car events. Strong EV presence in recent years. Every major manufacturer attends. Massively content-rich — hillclimb, reveals, celebrity appearances. Kyle Conner, MKBHD, and other major creators attend regularly.
IAA Munich
Top Priority 		Sept 2027
Biennial (odd years)		 Munich, Germany 🇩🇪 Europe's biggest auto show — the equivalent of Detroit for the European market. Heavily EV-focused since 2021. Next edition is 2027. Start building profile now to be credentialed then.
Paris Motor Show (Mondial de l'Auto)
Top Priority 		October 2026
Biennial		 Paris, France 🇫🇷 Returns October 2026 after its biennial cycle. Major global stage for concept cars and EV reveals. VW already confirmed. Strong content for nexusEVnews. ~1.5hr flight from Italy.
Brussels Motor Show
 January 2027
Annual		 Brussels, Belgium 🇧🇪 Europe's largest indoor auto show. European Car of the Year is announced here. 67% of vehicles on display are electrified. 2026 edition already passed — plan for January 2027. Apply by November 2026.
Essen Motor Show
 Nov–Dec 2026
Annual		 Essen, Germany 🇩🇪 Performance, tuning, and motorsport focus. Growing EV presence. Accessible press accreditation for independent outlets. Good end-of-year content.

Dates are based on best available information as of March 2026. Always verify current dates on each event's official website before booking travel.
