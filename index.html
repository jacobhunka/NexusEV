About the Curator

Jacob Hunka

Lifelong Sci-Fi reader. Former first mate on a racing vessel in St. Thomas. ESL teacher in Taipei. Level 1 Sommelier. Czech citizen living in Italy. At some point all of that wandering led to a Rivian R1T in the driveway and an obsession with where energy and transportation are headed.

Most recently I spent five years at Sticker Mule building and running a 31-person global support team — the kind of work that teaches you to cut through noise and find what actually matters. That instinct is what drives the curation here. No fluff, no press releases dressed up as news. Just the stuff worth your time.

I've owned a Rivian R1T Gen 1 and an Audi e-tron GT, so when this site covers either of those, I'm not reading from the outside.
